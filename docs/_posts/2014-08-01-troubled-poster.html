Over the summer between my Junior and Senior years of college, I decided to take on the challenge of making a large-scale hand-lettered piece based on one of my favorite scriptures, 2 Corinthians 4:8–9. After many nights after work spent locked in my room, hunched over my desk sketching, erasing, refining, inking, and finally vectorizing, this is what I came up with.
