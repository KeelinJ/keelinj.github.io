One of the projects I got to take on at TheBlaze was rapidly prototyping a logo and full site layout for a forthcoming tech blog to be named Pyrotech. The type in the logo is based on Chicago, one of the bitmapped fonts included with the original Macintosh. The 8-bit look is carried through to the type treatment. The rest of the site is a clean, modern layout featuring Open Sans in a variety of weights. The design was approved and we built out the site, but it has yet to be publicly launched.
